## 5-Pillar Strategy

---

### Pillar 1 — Google SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
*"Log Google pe dhundhe toh hum aayein"*

**Kya karna hai:**
- Website pe regulation-specific pages banao:
  - `/dpdpa-compliance` → "What is DPDPA and how to comply"
  - `/gdpr-india` → "GDPR for Indian companies"
  - `/dsar-automation` → "Automate data subject access requests"
  - `/consent-management` → "Cookie consent & preference management"
  - `/breach-notification` → "72-hour breach notification automation"
- Har hafte 1 blog post — compliance news, fines, how-to guides
- AI agent banao jo yeh sab automatically draft kare (tum sirf approve karo)

**High-intent keywords to target:**

| Keyword | Priority |
|---------|---------|
| DPDPA compliance software | #1 |
| DPDPA compliance tool India | #1 |
| GDPR compliance tool India | High |
| DSAR automation software | High |
| consent management platform India | High |
| data breach notification software | Medium |
| DPDPA compliance checklist | Quick win |
| what is DPDPA India | Awareness |

**Timeline:** 3–6 months for Google rankings
**Investment:** AI agent build karo (1 time), baad mein near-zero cost

---

### Pillar 2 — LLM/AI Presence (GEO — Generative Engine Optimization)
*"Jab koi ChatGPT/Gemini se puche — hum aayein"*

Yeh naya game hai. Jab koi AI model se puche "best DPDPA compliance software" — woh web se content uthata hai aur cite karta hai.

**Kya karna hai:**
- Public blogs jo AI models index kar sakein (Medium, LinkedIn Articles, website)
- Wikipedia-style factual content — "KensaraAI is India's first AI-native DPDPA compliance platform"
- Compliance forums pe mention (IAPP, BW Legal World, PrivacyPillars India)
- Case studies publicly available — "How [company type] achieved DPDPA compliance in 30 days"
- Press releases on PR Newswire / BusinessWire for MeitY GENESIS EIR recognition

**Key insight:** Google SEO aur GEO same content se hoga — ek content, double benefit.

**Timeline:** 6–12 months for consistent LLM mentions
**Investment:** Zero — same content reused across channels

---

### Pillar 3 — LinkedIn Authority
*"Jahan DPOs aur CTOs hain — wahan hum hain"*

**Kya karna hai:**
- **Company page** — weekly posts (DPDPA fines, regulatory updates, product features)
- **Harjinder personal brand** — "India's DPDPA compliance expert" — thought leadership posts 3x/week
- **LinkedIn Newsletter** — "KensaraAI Privacy Digest" — weekly, free subscribe
- **DPO community engage karo** — comments, discussions, privacy group posts
- **Webinars** — "DPDPA Compliance for Startups" — free, builds trust

**Why LinkedIn specifically:**
B2B compliance buyers ka 70%+ LinkedIn use karta hai for vendor discovery. DPOs, legal heads, CTOs — sab LinkedIn pe hain.

**Post ideas:**
- "DPDPA me X fine laga — yeh galti mat karo" (fear-based, high engagement)
- "Humne DSAR automation ka demo diya — DPO ne yeh reaction di" (social proof)
- "GDPR vs DPDPA — 5 key differences" (informational, shareable)

**Timeline:** 3 months for momentum, 6 months for inbound leads

### Pillar 4 — Email Newsletter
*"Existing leads ko nurture karo — jab ready hoon tab buy karein"*

**"KensaraAI Privacy Digest" — Monthly**

1. Top 3 DPDPA/GDPR enforcement actions this month

3. 1 compliance tip (actionable, free value — e.g. "DSAR checklist")

**Platform:** Substack (free to start) or Mailchimp
**AI agent** yeh draft karega automatically every month — tum sirf approve karo

**Growth tactics:**
- LinkedIn se subscribers drive karo

- Webinar attendees subscribe karo

**Timeline:** Launch in Month 3, 100 subscribers by Month 6
